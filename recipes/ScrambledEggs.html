Scrambled Eggs

A classic breakfast staple!

For millenia, people have been eating eggs. You can cook them so many ways, but scrambling is my favorite!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Turn stove on medium heat, and set down pan.
  2. Butter pan.
  3. Crack eggs into pan.
  4. Stir eggs, until they begin to get firm.
  5. Add salt and pepper, and continue to cook until no longer runny.
