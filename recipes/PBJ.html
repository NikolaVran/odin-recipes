PB&J

A kid's classic!

The PB&J is a kid's classic. It takes no time to prepare, and the kids love it! No longer will the kids go hungry!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Take out two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  4. Combine slices of bread to make sandwhich.
